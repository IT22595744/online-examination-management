Educational Methods

Education is the process of facilitating learning, or the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, morals, beliefs, and habits. Educational methods include teaching, training, storytelling, discussion, and targeted research. Education frequently takes place under the guidance of educators, but learners may also educate themselves. Education can take place in formal or informal settings and any experience that has a formative effect on how one thinks, feels, or acts may be considered educational. The teaching methodology is frequently determined by the environment in which it takes place and the needs of the learner.
